How Bad Is The California Drought?

Click the reservoirs on the map above to see a history of water levels.

Reservoirs are only one measurement of drought in California, but looking at their water levels over time can give us context for its severity. If you want a more complete picture, look at the resources below. And yeah, it's really bad.
